## Overview

After a lengthy hiatus, Beorn's Path has returned for Part 15. This time around we are exploring [[The Seventh Level]] of Khazad-dûm with [[Dain Ironfoot]] and his trusty army of dwarves.

This entry is presented as a video playthrough rather than a written session report. As Beorn explains:

> By using video instead of written articles, I can avoid the lengthy process of transcribing and editing session reports for each scenario.

**Note on video quality:** The video is a bit cut off on the lower right side — Beorn acknowledged these technical difficulties and noted he would smooth them out as the series continued.

**Video:** [Watch Beorn's Path 15 – The Seventh Level on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oDfVi8SfOOY)
