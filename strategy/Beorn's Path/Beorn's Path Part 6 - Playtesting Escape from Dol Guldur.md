## Overview

This is a detailed playthrough of [[Escape from Dol Guldur]], the most challenging core set scenario, using two decks played in two-handed mode (simulating two players). The author emphasizes that "the harder a scenario is, the more you have to gain from an aggressive strategy."

[[Escape from Dol Guldur]] is "particularly ill-suited for solo play" due to the mechanic of losing one hero at the outset to imprisonment — the player must account for operating at reduced power during the earliest, most critical rounds. The two-deck approach is essential because single-hero imprisonment cripples any single-deck strategy.

Playing quickly through quest stages is the core philosophy here: the faster you advance, the fewer encounter cards you accumulate and the less your threat spirals out of control.

## Round 1 — Stage 1B (0 of 9 progress)

### Planning Phase

- Tactics/Spirit passes planning phase entirely, conserving resources.
- Leadership/Lore plays [[Snowbourn Scout]] (1 resource from [[Theodred]]), triggering its ability to place progress on [[Tower Gate]] and explore it — this immediately removes [[Tower Gate]] from the staging area and makes [[Gandalf's Map]] claimable.

### Quest Phase

Both decks commit heroes to questing:
- Tactics/Spirit commits: [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]], [[Gimli]]
- Leadership/Lore commits: [[Denethor]], [[Theodred]]

**Encounter cards revealed:**
1. [[Dol Guldur Beastmaster]] — takes 1 damage from [[Thalin]]'s ability (enemies that enter the staging area take 1 damage).
2. [[The Necromancer's Reach]] — treachery: all ready characters take 1 damage. Fortunately, all characters are committed to the quest (exhausted), so no damage is dealt.

**Quest resolution:** 9 willpower vs. 3 threat in staging area = **6 progress tokens** placed on Stage 1B (need 9 total).

### Combat Phase

- Tactics/Spirit optionally engages [[Dungeon Jailor]] (removing a 3-threat enemy from the staging area).
- Leadership/Lore optionally engages the wounded [[Dol Guldur Beastmaster]].

**Shadow cards:**
- [[Dungeon Jailor]]'s shadow: [[Eyes of the Forest]] — harmless as a shadow effect.
- [[Dol Guldur Beastmaster]]'s shadow: [[Caught in a Web]] and [[Tower Gate]] — notably, [[Caught in a Web]] as a shadow effect negates its harmful when-revealed effect (a lucky break).

**Attacks and defense:**
- [[Gimli]] takes 2 damage from an undefended [[Dungeon Jailor]] attack.
- [[Snowbourn Scout]] defends the [[Dol Guldur Beastmaster]] but is killed.
- Leadership/Lore plays [[Valiant Sacrifice]] (1 resource from [[Theodred]]) in response to the Scout's death, drawing 2 cards: [[Sneak Attack]] and [[Gleowine]].

### End of Round

- Both decks increase threat by 1.

## Round 2 — Stage 1B (6 of 9 progress)

### Planning Phase

- Leadership/Lore is now first player.
- Tactics/Spirit attaches [[Citadel Plate]] to [[Gimli]] (spending all available resources from dwarf heroes), raising his total hit points to 9. This is the critical setup that makes [[Gimli]] capable of tanking the [[Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] later.
- Leadership/Lore passes on playing anything to conserve resources.

### Quest Phase

- Tactics/Spirit: [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]]
- Leadership/Lore: [[Theodred]], [[Denethor]] — [[Theodred]] triggers his response to give [[Denethor]] a resource.

**Encounter cards revealed:**
1. [[Cavern Guardian]] — takes 1 damage from [[Thalin]].
2. [[Dol Guldur Orcs]] — takes 1 damage from [[Thalin]]; its when-revealed effect deals 2 damage to [[Theodred]], who is now at 1 hit point remaining.

**Quest resolution:** 7 willpower vs. 4 threat = **3 progress**, completing Stage 1B (total: 9 of 9). Players claim the now-unguarded [[Gandalf's Map]] and raise their threat by 2 (the cost of claiming an objective). Stage advances to **Stage 2: Through the Caverns**.

### Combat Phase

- Tactics/Spirit engages [[Dungeon Jailor]] (no ready defender available in that deck).
- Leadership/Lore engages [[Cavern Guardian]]; [[Snowbourn Scout]] defends.

**Shadow cards:**
- [[Dungeon Jailor]]'s shadow: [[Ungoliant's Spawn]] — a significant 8-threat penalty for an undefended attack.
- [[Cavern Guardian]]'s shadow: [[Endless Caverns]] — no meaningful effect.

**Attacks:**
- [[Gimli]] counterattacks with 9 attack damage, killing [[Dungeon Jailor]] outright.

### End of Round

- Both decks increase threat by 1.

## Round 3 — Stage 2B (0 of 15 progress)

Stage 2: "Through the Caverns" requires 15 progress and only one ally may be played per round between both decks combined.

### Planning Phase

- Tactics/Spirit plays [[Galadhrim's Greeting]] (3 resources from [[Eowyn]]), reducing Tactics/Spirit threat to 36 — important breathing room.
- Leadership/Lore plays [[Daughter of Nimrodel]] (3 resources from [[Denethor]]) — she will be the primary healing engine for the rest of the game.
- Tactics/Spirit is first player.

### Quest Phase

- Tactics/Spirit: [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]]
- Leadership/Lore: [[Theodred]]
- Both players discard cards to trigger [[Eowyn]]'s willpower boost: Tactics/Spirit discards [[Dwarven Axe]]; Leadership/Lore discards [[Guard of the Citadel]].

**Encounter cards revealed:**
1. [[Under the Shadows]] — adds 2 threat to the staging area.
2. [[Hummerhorns]] — takes 1 damage from [[Thalin]].

**Quest resolution:** 8 willpower vs. 5 threat = **3 progress**. 

As a result of reaching 3 progress, [[Aragorn]] is freed from prison and enters play — but takes 1 damage when freed. Additionally, [[The Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] is added to the staging area as an immediate threat.

### Combat Phase

- Tactics/Spirit does not optionally engage the [[Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] (it remains in the staging area, adding threat but not attacking yet).
- Leadership/Lore exhausts [[Denethor]] to defend against [[Dol Guldur Orcs]].

**Shadow cards:**
- [[Cavern Guardian]]'s shadow: [[Great Forest Web]] — no effect.
- [[Dol Guldur Orcs]]' shadow: [[Enchanted Stream]] — no effect.

**Attacks:**
- [[Gimli]] and [[Aragorn]] counterattack together, killing both engaged enemies.
- [[Daughter of Nimrodel]] heals 2 damage on [[Gimli]] at end of round.

### End of Round

- Both decks increase threat by 1.

## Round 4 — Stage 2B (5 of 15 progress — including progress from Round 3 resolution)

### Planning Phase

- Leadership/Lore is now first player.
- Tactics/Spirit plays [[Gondorian Spearman]] (2 resources from [[Gimli]]) and attaches [[Blade of Gondolin]] to [[Gimli]] (1 resource from [[Thalin]]).
- Leadership/Lore passes (conserving resources).

### Quest Phase

- Leadership/Lore: [[Gandalf]] via [[Sneak Attack]] (1 resource from [[Theodred]]), [[Aragorn]], [[Theodred]] — [[Theodred]] gives a resource to [[Aragorn]] who readies via the generated resource.
- Tactics/Spirit: [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]]
- Before the quest phase resolves, [[Gandalf]] uses his enter-play ability to deal **4 damage** to [[The Nazgul of Dol Guldur]].

**Encounter cards revealed:**
1. [[Dol Guldur Orcs]] — [[Gandalf]] takes 2 damage from the when-revealed effect (he has entered play via [[Sneak Attack]]).
2. [[Chieftain Ufthak]] — a formidable new enemy enters the staging area.

**Quest resolution:** Players discard [[For Gondor!]] and [[Veteran Axehand]] to give [[Eowyn]] +2 willpower. Total: 14 willpower vs. 10 threat = **4 progress** (running total: 9 of 15).

### Combat Phase

- Leadership/Lore engages [[Chieftain Ufthak]]; [[Denethor]] defends.
- Tactics/Spirit engages [[Dol Guldur Orcs]]; [[Aragorn]] defends (using his Sentinel ability to defend for the other player's deck).

**Shadow cards:**
- [[Chieftain Ufthak]]'s shadow: [[Under the Shadow]] — no effect; but [[Chieftain Ufthak]]'s forced effect triggers, making him attack for 5 next round.
- [[Dol Guldur Orcs]]' shadow: [[King Spider]] — exhausts [[Gondorian Spearman]].

**Attacks:**
- [[Gimli]] counterattacks, killing [[Dol Guldur Orcs]] and triggering [[Blade of Gondolin]] for +1 progress token.
- [[Daughter of Nimrodel]] heals 2 damage on [[Theodred]].

### End of Round

- Both decks increase threat by 1.

## Round 5 — Stage 2B (10 of 15 progress)

### Planning Phase

- Tactics/Spirit is now first player.
- Tactics/Spirit attaches [[Unexpected Courage]] to [[Gimli]] (2 resources from [[Eowyn]]) — this allows [[Gimli]] to both defend and attack in the same round.
- Leadership/Lore attaches [[Steward of Gondor]] to [[Denethor]] (2 resources from [[Aragorn]]), then immediately exhausts it to generate 2 resources — now [[Denethor]] has a formidable 5 resources total.
- Leadership/Lore plays [[Forest Snare]] on [[Chieftain Ufthak]] (3 resources from [[Denethor]]), neutralizing him completely — he cannot attack while ensnared.
- Leadership/Lore plays [[Gandalf]] via [[Sneak Attack]] again (spending 5 resources total: 3 from [[Theodred]], 2 from [[Denethor]]), dealing 4 more damage to [[The Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] — the Nazgul now has very little health remaining.

### Quest Phase

- Tactics/Spirit: [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]], [[Gimli]] (readies via [[Unexpected Courage]] after questing)
- Leadership/Lore: [[Theodred]], [[Aragorn]], [[Gandalf]]
- [[Theodred]] gives a resource to [[Eowyn]].

**Encounter cards revealed:**
1. [[Mountains of Mirkwood]] — a location enters the staging area.
2. [[Endless Caverns]] — location with Doomed 1; surges, revealing [[Enchanted Stream]] as well.

**Quest resolution:** Players discard [[Henamarth Riversong]] and [[Northern Tracker]] to boost [[Eowyn]] by +2. Total: 15 willpower vs. 11 threat = **4 progress**, reaching exactly 15 progress needed to complete Stage 2B.

> Strategic note: The players deliberately hold off on claiming the remaining objectives ([[Shadow Key]] and [[Dungeon Torch]]) until after the quest phase resolves, to avoid having their threat penalty counted toward the staging area threat during questing.

### Combat Phase

- Tactics/Spirit optionally engages [[The Nazgul of Dol Guldur]].
- [[Gondorian Spearman]] defends [[The Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] and triggers his response ability — he deals 1 damage to the attacker before it attacks, which combined with prior Gandalf damage is enough to kill the Nazgul before it can deal any damage.
- [[Chieftain Ufthak]] is trapped by [[Forest Snare]] and cannot attack.
- Both enemies' shadow cards are discarded without resolution.

**Claiming objectives:**
- Leadership/Lore raises threat by 4 to claim [[Shadow Key]] (attached to [[Aragorn]]) and [[Dungeon Torch]] (attached to [[Theodred]]).
- [[Daughter of Nimrodel]] heals 2 damage on [[Aragorn]].

### End of Round

- Threat increases per deck.
- [[Aragorn]] takes 1 damage from [[Shadow Key]]'s ongoing effect.
- [[Dungeon Torch]] raises threat by 2.
- Stage advances to **Stage 3B: "Out of the Dungeons"** (need 7 progress).

## Round 6 — Stage 3B (0 of 7 progress)

### Planning Phase

- Leadership/Lore is first player.
- Leadership/Lore plays [[Gleowine]] (2 resources from [[Denethor]]) for card draw.
- Tactics/Spirit plays [[Gandalf]] from hand (5 resources), using his ability to immediately kill [[Hummerhorns]] upon entering play.

### Stage 3B Special Mechanic — Orc Guards

Each deck places its top card face-down as an "Orc Guard" engaged against it — these are 1 attack / 1 defense / 1 hit point enemies that must be dealt with each round.

### Quest Phase

- Leadership/Lore: [[Aragorn]], [[Theodred]], [[Gleowine]]
- Tactics/Spirit: [[Gandalf]], [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]], [[Gimli]] (readies via [[Unexpected Courage]])

**Encounter cards revealed:**
1. [[The Necromancer's Reach]] — deals 1 damage to [[Gandalf]], [[Thalin]], [[Eowyn]], [[Theodred]], and [[Gleowine]]; [[Gimli]] and [[Aragorn]] are unaffected (they were already exhausted / readied). [[Gandalf]] is now at 1 hit point remaining.
2. [[Dol Guldur Orcs]] — deals 2 damage to [[Gandalf]], reducing him to 0 hit points and removing him from play.

**Quest resolution:** 15 willpower vs. 7 threat = **8 progress**, exceeding the 7 needed on Stage 3B.

**Victory!**

[[Daughter of Nimrodel]] heals 2 damage on [[Theodred]] before the final tally.

## Key Strategic Takeaways

### Aggression Over Caution
The harder a scenario is, the more you have to gain from an aggressive strategy. In [[Escape from Dol Guldur]], stalling is fatal — threat accumulates, encounter cards pile up, and the restricted ally-play rule on Stages 1 and 2 means you never build the board state needed to recover.

### [[Gimli]] as Primary Tank
Equipping [[Citadel Plate]] on [[Gimli]] as early as possible (Round 2 here) is critical. His 9 hit points make him capable of absorbing serious punishment from the [[Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] and other heavy hitters. [[Unexpected Courage]] then frees him to both defend and attack in the same round.

### [[Sneak Attack]] + [[Gandalf]] as a Repeatable Tool
Using [[Sneak Attack]] to bring [[Gandalf]] into play allows you to deal 4 damage to a key enemy (the Nazgul), reduce threat, or draw cards — then [[Gandalf]] returns to your hand at the end of the round. This was used twice: in Round 4 and Round 5, dealing 8 total damage to [[The Nazgul of Dol Guldur]] before he was finished off.

### [[Eowyn]]'s Discard Ability — Use It Aggressively
Discarding cards from hand for +1 willpower each is a powerful tempo play when you need to push through quest stages quickly. In this game, multiple cards were discarded across multiple rounds to make key progress thresholds.

### [[Forest Snare]] as a Combat Bypass
[[Forest Snare]] on [[Chieftain Ufthak]] completely neutralized a 5-attack enemy that would have been devastating. This freed up defenders and attackers to focus on the Nazgul and Orc Guards.

### Accepting Undefended Attacks Early
During Stages 1 and 2, with only one ally allowed per round, undefended attacks are sometimes unavoidable. The key is to build [[Gimli]]'s hit point buffer so that the player deck with more combat resources can tank hits without losing heroes.

### Timing of Objective Claims
Wait to claim objectives with threat-raising penalties ([[Shadow Key]], [[Dungeon Torch]]) until after questing resolves in the round you advance. This prevents the extra threat from inflating the staging area during the quest phase.

### [[Daughter of Nimrodel]] — Sustain Engine
[[Daughter of Nimrodel]]'s 2 healing per round is essential for keeping damaged heroes (especially [[Gimli]] and [[Theodred]]) alive across six rounds of intense combat.
